What is Vaiter

Mobile App

Vaiter is a powerful mobile application built to tackle social distancing during Covid-19.

Catered for students

Waiting in long queues to buy food while social distancing is a tough task for students and thats where Vaiter comes in.

Easy to use

Simply order your food at your favourite university restaurant and pick it up on your own busy schedule.

Vaiter in a nutshell

With Covid-19 showing no signs of regression, it is of paramount importance that we practise social distancing as a society. Shops and restaurants being crowded is an inevitability, but it does not necessarily need to be a risk. Many times, the root of a crowd at university shops is the long queues of students waiting to buy their lunch or simply a snack. This app offers a way to eliminate that factor completely, by students having placed an order beforehand with ease.
